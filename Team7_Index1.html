Who we are

A sneak peek into our world!

Travel Experts was incorporated in 1958 by Robert McKinsy in Calgary, Alberta. He recognized the need for Calgarians to take a break off work to recuperate and rejuvenate thier minds and body. He also discovered that tourist sites in Alberta were always desolate due to lack of awareness of thier existence. In catering to these needs, he established Travel Experts to organize tours and vacation for Calgarians and Canadians at large.
